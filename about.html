Theresa Pacheco is a California licensed Certified Public Accountant. She graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, major in Accounting. Theresa gained her Master's Degree major in Finance in 2004.Her experience in public accounting is more than twenty years.

Her audit include the following industries - Non-Profit Organizations, United States Housing and Urban Development Agency (HUD) funded housing projects, Independent Physician Association (IPA's), Employees' Benefits Plan & 401(k) and For-Profit businesses.

Theresa is active with her charity work. Her mother taught her the lesson of caring, especially to those who are in need.

During summer vacations, she enjoys discovering new food and learning local cultures with family and friends. She likes to visit museums, parks, gardens and taking short hikes with her camera. She is an advocate of Go Green.
